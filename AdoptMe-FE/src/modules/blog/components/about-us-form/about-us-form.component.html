Who we are?


Adopt me is a free app that connect between rescued pets to adopters.
We collect pets data from the nearby shelters and display all available pets here in order to help you choose your new best friend!
You can find all the pet in our pets section and also to add a pet to our database.
We are non-profit organization and always shorts in volunteers, so if you want to help use you can fill your details in the volunteer section.
